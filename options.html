Big YouTube Text Grabber - Instructions

  1. Go to a YouTube video and pause it at a frame containing text that you want to copy.
  2. Click the "BTG" button, which should appear near the top-left corner of the video player.
  3. Click and drag your mouse to draw a rectangle around the text you want to copy. Release the mouse button once you've made your selection.
  4. The extension will process the selected area, recognize the text, and automatically copy it to your clipboard. The button will turn pale-green to indicate success.
  5. Paste the copied text wherever you want to use it.
